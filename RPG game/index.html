Lost Soldier

Story

You are a soldier who has just woken up from a coma in an abondoned world and left a camp in search of your family. You are on the way towards a city center, what troubles will you encounter?

Instructions

  • To move forward in the game you must click on one of the options displayed in the white boxes.
  • Some papes will have sound effects that can be played and paused.
  • Some pages have multiple choices which will lead to different endings.
  • There will also be items and memoires(grey boxes) to collect throughout which will help determine your ending.

Press START to begin
